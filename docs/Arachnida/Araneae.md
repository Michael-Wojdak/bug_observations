### Dark Fishing Spider
*Dolomedes tenebrosus*

Like [water striders](../Insects/Hemiptera.html#water-strider), fishing spiders are able to float on the water's surface using water tension. They hunt along the edges of bodies of water, capturing prey that is trapped on the surface. While this species tends to be more arboreal and less dependent on aquatic habitats, this individual was found only a few feet from the riverside.

<img src="../../assets/images/Arachnida/dark fishing spider.jpg" title="Southwest Virginia, June 2023" width="60%">
