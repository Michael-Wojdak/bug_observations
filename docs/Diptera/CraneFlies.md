# Crane Flies

The common name of crane fly refers to several distinct groups of flies that are lumped together due to similar appearences.

## Typical Crane Flies

Typical crane flies are members of the superfamily Tipuloidea. They include the Cylindrotomidae, Limoniidae, Pediciidae, and Tipulidae families.
