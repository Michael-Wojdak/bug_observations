# Ants

Alates are reproductive, winged ants that disperse from the colony to mate and form new colonies.
<img src="{{site.baseurl}}/assets/images/Hymenoptera/Alate Pair.jpg" title="Southwest Virginia, Aug. 2025" width="60%">

Ants can regurgitate food to share with other individuals from the colony.
